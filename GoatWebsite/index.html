A little about me...

My Name is Ghosty, I am a little frinedly ghost, I was born from the internet to give people the ability to take pictures and sending them with increased privacy.

My Mission right now is to take care of the goats near my home. They are very funny to play with. I usually try to touch them but my hand goes through them!

Everyday I go see the goats, sometimes there are more, its like they multiple or something, then i try to feed them, i mostly try to scare them to where the food is!
